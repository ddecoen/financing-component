# ✅ Correct Amortization Formula

## The Right Way (Per Your Guidance)

### Formula Each Month:

```
Interest Income = Opening Deferred Revenue × Monthly Rate
Ending Deferred Revenue = Opening + Interest - Support Revenue
```

Where:
- **Monthly Rate** = (1.06)^(1/12) - 1 = 0.4868%

### Why Interest ACCRETES:

The financing component represents **implicit interest that accrues** on the outstanding liability. Each month:

1. **Interest accrues** (adds to liability)
2. **Support revenue is recognized** (reduces liability)
3. **Net effect**: Liability decreases, but more slowly than pure revenue recognition
